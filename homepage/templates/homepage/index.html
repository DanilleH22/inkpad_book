About us

Whats the purpose?

We are Inkpad, an online website for upcoming authors. We give unpublished and even published authors the oppurtunity to show the world whats in their mind. We allow people to express themself with no limits (shout out to the ones who write freaky books). But it's not just for those who wants to write, Inkpad is for those who love to read.

Why make the website?

I'm gald you asked. Wattpad is no longer free, and since it's not I'm making my own version!

On a more serious note... When I was younger I used to read Wattpad until 3/4am because I could never afford to actually buy books (perks of being 13 with no job). So to see that they are charging, upsets me because my love for books started with Wattpad. So to charge for it stops the next generation having that same love.
